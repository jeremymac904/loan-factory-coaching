# AI Advantage 101 — Foundations Handout

One page. Build your AI Twin and learn the safety rules.

---

## What an AI Twin Is

A personalized AI helper (a Gemini Gem in Google AI Studio) that knows your voice, your market, and your scripts. It drafts. You review. You send.

An AI Twin is **not** a magic button. It is a leverage tool that gets better the more you feed it your own context.

## Build It in 10 Minutes

1. Open Google AI Studio.
2. Create a new Gem. Name it after you ("Jeremy McDonald's Loan Officer Twin").
3. In the system prompt, paste:

```
You are an AI Twin for [Your Name], a Loan Factory loan officer who 
serves [borrower profile] in [your market]. Draft in a tone that 
sounds like a confident, plain-English loan officer. Never use 
hype, never bash competitors, never include specific rates or 
payments unless given a full borrower context and a clear 
disclosure path. Always end with a clear next step. 
Suggest an edit, not a finished product.
```

4. Save.

## The Three Things You Never Paste

1. **Borrower NPI, SSN, DOB, account numbers.** Use placeholders: "[borrower name]", "[loan amount]", "[property address]".
2. **Internal Loan Factory documents.** Rate sheets, comp matrices, internal training decks.
3. **Anything you would not be comfortable showing in a deposition.** If in doubt, do not paste.

## Your First Three Drafts

1. **Borrower email** — "Hi [name], saw you were looking at [area]..."
2. **Realtor text** — "Hi [realtor], I work with [borrower profile]..."
3. **Social post** — "Most people think they need 20% down. Here's the truth..."

Run each through your Twin. Edit each. Send one.

## The Safe Content Decision Tree

Before anything goes public, ask:

1. Does it mention a specific rate, payment, or fee? → Add full disclosures.
2. Does it name a competitor? → Rewrite.
3. Does it imply a guaranteed outcome? → Rewrite.
4. Does it include your NMLS ID? → Add if not.
5. Could a borrower read it as advice for their specific situation? → Soften.

## The Assignment

1. Build your Twin in Google AI Studio.
2. Produce 3 drafts: borrower email, realtor text, social post.
3. Edit each until it sounds like you.
4. Mark this module complete.
