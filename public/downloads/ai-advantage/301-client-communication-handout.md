# AI Advantage 301 — Client Communication Handout

One page. First touches in under 60 seconds.

---

## The 60 Second Draft Loop

1. Open your Twin.
2. Paste the borrower / realtor / past client context.
3. Ask: "Draft a [text | email | call script] in my tone. Keep it under [N] words. No specific rates or payments. End with a clear next step."
4. Read the draft.
5. Edit. Send.

If the draft is more than 60 seconds from prompt to send, you are editing too much. The Twin needs better context.

## Five First Touches for This Week

| # | Type | Context to give the Twin |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Borrower first email | Name, scenario, source of lead, timeline |
| 2 | Realtor follow up | Agent name, brokerage, recent listing, your value offer |
| 3 | Past client check in | Name, close date, last touch, tenure bucket |
| 4 | Loan status update | Borrower name, current stage, next milestone |
| 5 | Rate shopper response | Rate they saw, what they care about, your value offer |

## The Edit Rules

Always edit before sending. The Twin is a draft, not a final.

- Replace placeholder names with real names.
- Add one specific detail the Twin could not have known.
- Remove anything that sounds like "AI wrote this."
- Check for: hype, bashing, guarantees, missing NMLS ID.

## The Gmail + Workspace Pattern

If you use Gmail and Google Workspace:

1. Open the email draft in Gmail.
2. Open your Twin in a separate tab.
3. Ask the Twin to draft a reply to the email.
4. Paste the draft into Gmail.
5. Edit. Send.
