# AI Advantage 201 — Twin Setup Handout

One page. Make your Twin yours.

---

## Why Sources Matter

A generic AI Twin drafts like a textbook. A Twin with your sources drafts like a sharper, faster you. Sources are the difference between a draft you'd never send and a draft you edit in 30 seconds.

## Connect 3 Sources

Pick three from this list and connect them to your Twin in Google AI Studio:

1. **Scripts folder.** Your existing scripts from `/resources?tab=scripts`. Upload the markdown files to your Drive folder, then connect.
2. **Past client list.** A spreadsheet of your past clients (name, close date, last touch, tenure bucket). Connect as a Gem source.
3. **Bio doc.** A short markdown doc with: who you are, your market, your borrower profile, your tone, your NMLS ID. 1 page max.

Optional fourth: your **calendar template** (theme days, daily time blocks).

## Bio Doc Template

```
# [Your Name] — AI Twin Bio

## Who I am
[One paragraph. Who you serve, your market, your borrower profile.]

## My tone
[Three adjectives. e.g., confident, plain-English, never hype.]

## My market
[City, county, state, main borrower profile, top 3 partner categories.]

## Things I never say
[Specific rate quotes without context, competitor bashing, 
"guaranteed approval", "best rate", "free processing".]

## Things I always include
[NMLS ID, Equal Housing logo on social, plain-English explanations, 
a clear next step.]

## Compliance reminder
All drafts are LO reviewed. Compliance has the final say on 
anything that goes to a borrower or Realtor.
```

## Verify Your Sources

After connecting, re-run a draft you ran yesterday. The new draft should:

- Reference your actual scripts (not generic ones)
- Reference your market specifics
- Sound more like your tone

If it does not, your sources are not connected. Re-check.
