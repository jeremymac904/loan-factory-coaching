# 301 Referral Partner Growth — Partner Playbook

A one page reference for building strong, repeatable Realtor and referral partner relationships.

---

## Purpose

To build a steady engine of new referral partner conversations that turns into closed loans over 90 to 180 days.

## What Realtors Actually Care About

1. **Speed** — How fast can you pre-qual? How fast can you close?
2. **Communication** — Will you update them proactively, or wait until they ask?
3. **Closing certainty** — Will the deal fall apart at the last minute?
4. **Ease of working with you** — Are you hard or easy to reach?

Lead with these. Not with "I'm better than the other LO."

## Picking Five Priority Partners

Not 50. Five.

Pick 5 Realtors (or other partners) in your market who:
- Are active (last 6 months of transactions)
- Work a similar borrower profile to yours
- Have a reputation you respect
- You have at least 1 personal connection to (school, kid, gym, church, charity)

Then do public research: their recent sales, their content, their reviews, their farm area.

## The First Outreach Message

```
Hi [Realtor Name],

Saw your [recent listing / recent closing / farm area post about X]. 
[One specific detail about what you noticed — not a compliment, an observation.]

I work with [borrower profile] buyers in [your area] through Loan Factory, 
and I close on time and keep everyone in the loop. I'd love to buy you 
coffee and learn what your buyers need most from a lender this year.

NMLS [ID].
```

Short. Specific. No begging. No bashing competitors.

## The First 15 Minute Partner Meeting

1. **5 min — Them.** Ask about their business. Their best month. Their worst month. What they're trying to fix this year.
2. **5 min — You.** What you do, who you serve, how you communicate on active deals. The best-price guarantee if relevant.
3. **5 min — Plan.** How you'd actually work together. One specific commitment from each side.

## Listing Agent Call (on every offer)

```
Hi [Listing Agent], I'm [LO] with Loan Factory, working with 
[Buyer] on [Property]. We're under contract, pre-qualified, and 
earnest money is in. 

A few quick questions: what's the seller's preferred closing date, 
are there any inspection contingencies I should know about, and 
is there anything I can do to make the title company's job easier?
```

Confident. Specific. Helpful.

## RESPA Section 8 Reminder

No things of value in exchange for referrals. No gift cards, paid subscriptions, or paid event tickets tied to sending business. Co-marketing requires both parties to pay pro rata, documented at fair market value. Any new MSA structure requires corporate approval.

## The Assignment

1. Pick 5 priority partners from your market.
2. Send 10 personalized partner outreaches this week.
3. Book at least 1 new partner conversation.
4. Make at least 1 listing agent call on an active file.

## Behavior Change

LO has 5 named priority partners and at least one active deal sourced from one of them.
