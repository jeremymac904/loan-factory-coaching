# 601 Elite Execution — Niche Plan Template

A one page reference for the 12 week niche plan you submit to graduate.

---

## Purpose

To graduate from "running around" to "running a system." You leave 601 with a written plan, a working AI prompt routine, and a measurable weekly scorecard.

## Pick One Niche

Not three. One.

| Niche | Why it works | Why you might pick it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Self-employed | Big borrower pool, hard to find a good LO | You speak the language |
| VA / military | Steady pipeline, deep relationships | You or family served |
| First-time buyer | High volume, big impact | Your market has first-timers |
| Investor | Repeat business, larger loans | You already know investors |
| Divorce | Time-sensitive, no marketing fluff | You have a partner who refers |
| New construction | Builder relationships, steady flow | Your market is growing |
| Reverse | Older homeowners, underserved | You have a 55+ community nearby |

Pick one. Commit for 90 days. Re-evaluate at the end.

## The 12 Week Plan (One Page)

```
Week 1-2:  [Foundation. Pick the niche. List 25 priority partners. 
            Open 3 source funnels. Write your AI prompt routine.]
Week 3-4:  [Outreach. Send 5 partner outreaches per week. Post 
            2 pieces of niche content. Make 1 listing agent call 
            per active file.]
Week 5-6:  [First wins. Book 2 partner meetings. Close or pre-qual 
            1 borrower. Get 1 past client referral.]
Week 7-8:  [Double down. Send 5 more outreaches. Post 3 pieces. 
            Make listing agent calls on every offer.]
Week 9-10: [Mid review. Audit pipeline. Cut what isn't working. 
            Double what is. Recommit or pivot niche.]
Week 11-12:[Final push. Pre-qual 3 more. Book 2 more partner 
            meetings. Submit your 12 week plan and your AI prompt 
            routine.]
```

## The AI Prompt Routine

Pick 5 prompts you actually use weekly. Document the input, the output, and the edit you make.

```
Prompt 1: [Pre-call brief]
Input: borrower name, scenario, last touch
Output: 3 things to know going in
Edit: I add 1 thing I already know

Prompt 2: [Follow up text]
... etc
```
