The core problem

Most LOs know what to do. The gap is execution.

Inconsistent activity

Calls, follow-up, partner touches, and content often depend on motivation instead of a visible weekly standard.

Scattered resources

Recordings, scripts, docs, trackers, and coaching notes are useful only if members can find and use them quickly.

Too little feedback

LOs need coaching loops that review real work: calls, pipeline, partner outreach, content, and follow-up.

The platform has to turn coaching into a weekly operating system.

Quarterly bootcamp

A quarterly 3-day bootcamp creates implementation momentum.

Each day is 2 hours. The goal is not theory; the goal is real work produced during the session.

Day 1 · Foundation

Sales rhythm, first conversations, follow-up, and the tracker standard members will use all quarter.

Day 2 · Growth

Realtor outreach, partner strategy, content plan, and practical roleplay reps.

Day 3 · Execution

Pipeline review, AI workflow setup, scorecard commitments, and next-30-day plan.
